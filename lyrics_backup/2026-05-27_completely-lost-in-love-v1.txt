[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fire burning in your eyes tonight
Shadows dancing softly in the fading light
Every single breath you take is pulling me so close
You are the only magic that I want the most

[Verse 1]
Midnight on the porch, stars are falling down
We left all the noise back inside the town
Whispers in the dark, skin against my skin
This is where the beautiful, slow burn begins

[Pre-Chorus]
Every touch is a spark in the cool night air
Running my fingers right through your hair
Tension is rising, a sweet kind of ache
You are the habit I just cannot break

[Chorus]
I am completely lost in love, wrapped in your glow
Moving so steady, taking it slow
Losing my mind when you look at me that way
I never want to leave, I only want to stay

[Post-Chorus]
Completely lost, completely yours
Caught in the tide of these midnight shores

[Verse 2]
Tasting the summer upon your soft lips
Feeling the warmth from your fingertips
Shadows are weaving a blanket for two
Nothing else matters, just me and just you

[Bridge]
Completely lost in love, my heart is laid bare
Breathing the sweetness of the midnight air
Time stops entirely when our bodies meet
Lost in the rhythm of a perfect heartbeat
Saeed is falling deeper with every single sigh
Caught in the magic of an open sky

[Spoken]
I can read the secrets hiding in your smile
Let us stay in this quiet forever and a while

[Final Chorus]
